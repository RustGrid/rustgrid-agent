# Code of conduct

RustGrid contributors must help maintain a respectful, professional, and harassment-free community.

We welcome people regardless of background, identity, experience, nationality, appearance, disability, religion, or personal characteristics. Constructive technical disagreement is welcome; personal attacks, harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, and publication of private information are not.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when conduct is unsafe or disruptive. Enforcement decisions should consider context, impact, repetition, and community safety.

Do not use public issues for sensitive conduct reports. Contact a listed
repository maintainer privately, or use GitHub's abuse-reporting channel when a
private maintainer channel is not available. Maintainers will acknowledge
project-specific reports within five business days and share the outcome with
the reporter when privacy permits.
